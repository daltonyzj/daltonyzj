3D Printed PLC Adapter [2025]


Objective: Design a 3D printed quick release adapter for a PLC to a DIN rail

Designed the latching mechanism to use a given sliding catch with SOLIDWORKS
Utilised extension compliant mechanism to clamp sliding catch onto DIN rail
Iterated different springs (compression, tension, leaf) to optimise performance and assembly ease
Applied design for additive manufacturing principles to allow for fully print-in-place design
